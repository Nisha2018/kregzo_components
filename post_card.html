The Dentist Now Comes to You

Have you ever found it troublesome to do your regular dental check-ups?

Most people would say yes, as it might require them to take half a day off work in order to make it to the dentist.

However, what if, instead of you going to visit the dentist, the dentist comes to visit you?

That's exactly what Henry the Dentist does! This startup provides a mobile dental practice for companies, so that employees no longer miss out on their check-up routines.

Check out this link to see how this cool startup takes the pain out of visiting the dentist!
