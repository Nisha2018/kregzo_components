Today, there are numerous applications of artificial intelligence in the consumer and business spaces, from Apple’s Siri to Google’s DeepMind.Understanding AI and Machine learning (MI) would be a difficult task but the use of AI in business would actually be better than the human mind. As we all know how computers work and at what rate they can process symbolic, mathematical, situational, strategic, tactical operations. AI would help us look at different perspectives and also eliminate the possibility of human error alongside making human work easier. AI is not just any program or software but something that can process at quicker rates and erase those mundane tasks slowing you down and also being effective and efficient while doing it. Its benefits for a business in all sectors from the basic to the most complex tasks for eg: Cyber security to, automation of labour work to the high future planning with giving almost accurate results, It is an form of art in technology. Two of the biggest places that benefit the most are Marketing and Supply chain. Both of these are used in every sector of the corporate world. AI is expected to make the a larger impact in the forthcoming years.
