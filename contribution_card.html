Today, there are numerous applications of artificial intelligence in the consumer and business spaces, from Apple’s Siri to Google’s DeepMind.Understanding AI and Machine learning (MI) would be a difficult task but the use of AI in business would actually be better than the human mind. As we all know how computers work and at what rate they can process symbolic, mathematical, situational, strategic, tactical operations. AI would help us look at different perspectives and also eliminate the possibility of human error alongside making human work easier.
